Request A Delivery

Requesting a delivery with Cargoit is quick and easy.
Follow these simple steps to get started.

Download the Cargoit App

Download the Cargoit App from the
IOS App store or the Goggle Play store.

Create an account

Create an account,accept Terms & conditions
and Login.

Enter Delivery Details

Provide the Recipient's name,phone
then search & select Drop-off location.

Choose your delivery vehicle type

Select a vehicle type best to transport your cargo/parcel
considering the size and safety of your cargo/parcel.

Confirm with Driver

A driver will accept the request & might call you
to confirm & get more details about the delivery.

Pick up & Payment

Driver will come pick up the parcel and payment,
pay the driver with cash or any other
method you have aggreed on.

Track Your Delivery

Sit back & relax!
Track your cargo in real-time every step
of the way..
